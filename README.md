# Project-PRITHVI
### AI-Driven Urban Heat Stress Prediction & Mitigation Decision Support System

Project-PRITHVI AI is an AI-powered geospatial decision support platform designed to help urban planners identify, analyze, and mitigate Urban Heat Island (UHI) effects using satellite imagery, meteorological observations, and machine learning.

Developed as part of the **ISRO Bharatiya Antariksh Hackathon 2026**, the project combines geospatial analytics with Artificial Intelligence to predict Land Surface Temperature (LST), detect urban heat hotspots, and simulate climate mitigation strategies before implementation.

---

## 🌍 Problem Statement

Rapid urbanization has replaced natural landscapes with buildings, roads, and concrete surfaces that absorb and retain heat, creating Urban Heat Islands (UHIs).

Traditional systems mainly visualize existing temperatures but do not help answer:

- Where will future heat hotspots emerge?
- What factors contribute most to rising temperatures?
- Which mitigation strategy will provide maximum cooling?
- How much temperature reduction can be achieved before implementation?

Project-PRITHVI AI addresses these challenges through AI-powered prediction and simulation.
